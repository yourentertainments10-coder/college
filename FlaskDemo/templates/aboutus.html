About DSEU

University Overview

Delhi Skill and Entrepreneurship University (DSEU) was established in 2020 by the Government of NCT of Delhi to equip students with world-class skill education to enable access to aspirational jobs and inculcate entrepreneurial mindset and entrepreneurship.

Quick Facts
  • Established: 2020
  • Location: Delhi, India
  • Students: 5000+
  • Faculty: 200+

Vision & Mission

Vision

To be a globally acclaimed university for skill, entrepreneurship and innovation.

Mission

To create a skilled workforce ready for the industries of today and tomorrow.
